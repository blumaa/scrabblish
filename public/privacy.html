Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Data We Collect

Scrabblish collects the following data to provide the game service:

  • Account information: Email address (via Supabase Auth) used for login.
  • Profile data: Username and avatar selection.
  • Game data: Moves, scores, and game state for active and completed games.
  • Push tokens: Device tokens for delivering push notifications, stored only when you enable notifications.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the game: authenticating your account, managing games with friends, and sending turn notifications when enabled.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Game data is visible only to players in the same game.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS). Push tokens are stored alongside your user account and deleted when you disable notifications.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Push notification tokens are removed immediately when you disable notifications.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@scrabblish.app.
